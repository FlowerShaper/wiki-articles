_Tera I/O_ is a doujin album by [かめりあ](/camellia) (Camellia) released on 2020-09-27 by the circle [かめるかめりあ](#). It's a commemorative work of 10 songs to celebrate reaching 100,000 followers on X. It came with a [YouTube premiere](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M3npCLBbg-s) making it the first album to be premiered and future CTCD albums would make premieres a tradition.

The theme is computers with Tera referring to the prefix "Tera-" being commonly found in storage metrics as space can be measured in terabytes and I/O being the common acronym in data transfer, Input/Output. Also many songs have the letters "I" and "O" replaced with "1" and "0" respectively which refers to computers working in the machine code, binary, made up of 1s and 0s. The unique references each track title makes is discussed more in each music article.
