Méng-tún Kung-fu Dà-luànwǔ (a.k.a. Moebuta Kunfu Dairanto) is an original song for SEGA's music game [CHUNITHM](https://chunithm.sega.jp/) by [かめりあ](/camellia) feat. [ななひら](#). It appears in CHUNITHM ALL JUSTICE COLLECTION episode III, CD 4 where Camellia adopts the alias Li Camellia (李 龟琍椏) as the song has a Chinese theme and title. The physical form of the collection released exclusive to Japan as an album of 4 CDs
